Welcome

This is the official project page of Bricks 3D on GitHub.

The game was originally created for Mozilla's Dev Derby challenge in June 2013, which focused on WebGL, and was judged as a runner up.

A demo can also be found on my personal website. The demo performs best in Chrome.
